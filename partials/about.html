About


Ranawara offers simple yet beautifully decorated rooms, spacious living areas and stunning views stretching over the garden, pool and Indian Ocean

The main villa comprimises a total of 46,000 sq. ft. of open plan living. The property features a library, TV room, bar area, open plan kitcken & dining room, al-fresco dining pavilion, spa as well as 100ft long swimming pool.

We resident team of staff who cater for your needs in a home-style service for the duration of your stay. On site we have our British General Manager, a chef and his cooking team as well as service, room and garden staff.
